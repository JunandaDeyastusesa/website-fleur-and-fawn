About Us

You can custom outfit in here

Fleur and Fawn celebrates the beauty of global flora and fauna through its lovingly crafted plushies, designed to provide comfort and companionship to people of all ages. Our mission is to create heartfelt confidants that heal, teach, and grow alongside their owners, emphasizing quality, sustainability, and cultural diversity. With customizable options for animals, scents, and textures, each plushie offers a unique sensory experience. Many come with enchanting storybooks that promote education and empathy, addressing loneliness in a world where connection is vital. At Fleur and Fawn, we believe everyone deserves the soothing embrace of a plush companion.
